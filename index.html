Our Story

As students at NCU we realised that there was an issue with the long lines and congestion in the cafeteria. We sought to resolve this issue in the wake of the pandemic and came up with this solution, InstaCafé. with this, there will be more order and functionality in the Cafe and this will greatly improve customer service.

Our Menu

NCU Cafeteria has a wide variety of food and supplies at your reach. We aim to help enforce the practice of social distancing within the cafeteria while reducing the congestion to the benefit of customers.
